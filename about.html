About Us

Welcome to UMAVIK—where style meets individuality.

At UMAVIK, we believe that accessories are not just add-ons; they are essential elements that elevate your look and express your unique personality. Founded in 2005 by Souvik Bosu Roy, our brand was born out of a passion for fashion and a desire to create statement pieces that inspire confidence and creativity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual can showcase their style through curated accessories that blend quality, innovation, and trend-setting designs. Our mission is to empower you to express yourself through our carefully crafted collections, whether you’re dressing for a casual day out or a glamorous evening.

Quality Craftsmanship

Each piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed and meticulously crafted. We source the finest materials and collaborate with skilled artisans to ensure that every accessory meets our high standards of quality and durability. From elegant jewelry to stylish bags and more, our pieces are made to last and stand out.

Our Collection

Our carefully curated selection features a wide range of accessories, including statement jewelry, trendy handbags, elegant scarves, and more. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to combine style, quality, and functionality, ensuring that you not only look good but feel great wearing our products. We take pride in sourcing premium materials and collaborating with skilled artisans who share our commitment to excellence.

Our Values

At UMAVIK, we believe in the importance of sustainability and ethical practices. We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental footprint and ensuring that our production processes are as eco-friendly as possible. By choosing our accessories, you’re not just making a fashion statement; you’re supporting a brand that values both people and the planet.

Join Our Community

We’re more than just a brand, we’re a community of fashion lovers who celebrate creativity and self-expression. Follow us on social media, join our newsletter, and stay inspired by the latest trends, styling tips, and exclusive offers.

Thank you for choosing UMAVIK.
Together, let’s embrace the art of accessorizing and make every outfit uniquely yours!
